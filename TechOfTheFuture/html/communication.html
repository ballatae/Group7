5G

By reducing data rates, performance, and mobility, 5G is projected to connect almost all embedded sensors. This results in an extremely compact and cost-effective connection..

Smartphones

A smartphone is a phone that can do more than just make and receive phone calls and text messages. Smartphones are computer-like in that they can surf the internet and run software programs.

Meta

The metaverse is the next step in the growth of social networking. Because the company's mission is to assist bring the metaverse to life, we're altering our name to reflect that dedication.

M2M

M2M stands for machine-to-machine and refers to any technology that allows networked devices to share data and conduct operations without the need for human interaction.
