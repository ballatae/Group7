Revolutionary Traffic

Solving our traffic problems

It's a very common thing in today's traffic to get stuck with hours, standing in one place, while being late for work or school. The people have normalized this as a part of their everyday lives and they don't know about a better alternative. We can all agree that having a car in today's society is simply a mandatory need in order to be more mobile and be able to finish our everyday tasks quicker and more effeciently. But what happens when 100.000 people get in their cars and try to go somewhere in the same time at the same city? Chaos happens. Implementing some of the solutions proposed for the future we might be over this chaos. Using autonomous vehicles, or even making them be able to fly, we would free the cities from the traffc junctions forever.

Future solutions

It is no secret that major companies from all over the world are already investing millions of dollars into the future of the traffic. One of the best solutions for it will be the self-driving cars (autonomous cars). They will have the ability to communicate with each other and start or stop at the same time at traffic lights which would be make the traffic flow much faster. Another solution would be the adaptive traffic signals. They work with the self-driving cars to understand and regulate traffic patterns. Though only in-use in a handful of places currently, these smart systems show great potential for managing traffic patterns and reducing congestion at different times of the day.
