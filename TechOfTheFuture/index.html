Colonisation

The long-term occupation of the International Space Station by rotating teams of astronauts, scientists, and medical experts has supplied us with a plethora of data to develop standards for keeping people alive and well in the harsh environment of space for extended periods of time. Several major efforts on Earth have attempted to replicate as closely as possible the circumstances of off-world settlement in order to test the limits of human endurance.
