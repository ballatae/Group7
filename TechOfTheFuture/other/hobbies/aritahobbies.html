Hobbies

  • Arts
    • Painting

      As my favourite past time activity, painting is my favourite hobby. My favourite mediums to do so are Acrylics and Oil Paints.

      I love customizing random objects, and painting on canvases and easels.

      Painting gradients and blending colours together are my fortes, but usually I like trying new things and ideas too.

    • Drawing

      Connecting to painting, I like drawing too.

      Usually sketching on sketchbooks, I like using a multitude of graphite pencils and also with black and white charcoal.
      I usually draw semi-realistic characters and people.

    • Music

      I listen to a multitude and a wide variety of genres. From alternative and indie, to rock, my favourite bands are Arctic Monkeys, Pink Floyd and Chase Atlantic.

      I listen a lot to artists from my home country, so recently I have mostly been listening to the new albums from them.

  • Reading
    • Poems

      My reading tendencies have been encouraged from a very young age. Since my grandfather always read me his own children's books, which mostly contained poems, they quickly became my favourite type of literature.

      My favourite authors now are Edgar Allan Poe and William Blake.

    • Novels

      I have read novels as soon as I could understand them. From simplified versions of more complex books for kids, reading novels was also quite encouraged by my family.

      I do not have favourite genres, rather preferring to read from all types of them.
